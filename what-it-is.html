The idea of this site came to me when I read a book with a bit provocative title “How to Read a Book” written by Mortimer Adler. I strongly believe that it is an exceptional book and everyone should read it one day. One of the main ideas of this book is that the best way of gaining understanding is the communication directly from those who first discovered an idea, in other words, reading the Great Books of all time. Adler provided the recommended reading list and I tried to show those books from Adler's list in a more artistic way, because the great books deserve it.


In case you want to learn more about author or his book, you can discover the links in a navigation section at the top of the page.
